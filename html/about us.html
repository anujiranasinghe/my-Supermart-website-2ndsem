History of SUPERmart

SUPERmart is a Sri Lankan grocery store that provides a wide variety of premium beauty items, fresh dairy products, and organic veggies. Because of our dedication to sustainability and quality, every item on our shelves is ethically sourced and shipped out to clients on time. The first SUPERmart location opened its doors in Colombo in 2000. We saw significant development throughout the years, which resulted in the extension and widening of our branches. We presently proudly run four branches, thoughtfully placed in three distinct nations across the globe. SUPERmart is still a thriving Colombo branch, and we also have branches in Kandy, London, and Melbourne. We continue to grow and adapt to meet the varied demands of our consumers all around the world.

Our Branches

Colombo, Sri Lanka

SUPERmart's Colombo store, which is situated in the centre of Colombo, is where the SUPERmart journey got its start back in 2000. Our Colombo store, which is also our first, has a wide selection of high-end beauty products, fresh dairy, and organic veggies. The branch is known for sustainability and excellent customer service, constantly evolving to meet client demands and maintain its commitment to quality.

Kandy, Sri Lanka

Our Kandy branch, the second SUPERmart branch in Sri Lanka, was also established to meet the increasing demand and provides our customers with the same extensive selection of high-end beauty products, fresh dairy products, and organic veggies. The Kandy branch is located in the cultural centre of Sri Lanka and is an essential part of our purpose to cater to the wide range of needs of our customers.

London, United Kingdom

The groundbreaking move to take SUPERmart outside of Sri Lanka's boundaries is represented by our London location. To satisfy the varied demands of our clients in the UK, it provides a broad range of high-end beauty products, fresh dairy products, and organic vegetables from Sri Lanka. Being our first overseas location, it demonstrates our dedication to offering products with ethical origins while meeting the needs of customers throughout the world.

Melbourne, Australia

The second SUPERmart location outside of Sri Lanka, our Melbourne location is well-positioned to cater to the varied needs of the community by providing an extensive range of high-quality beauty products, fresh dairy products, and organic veggies that are sourced responsibly and delivered on time. Since its founding, the Melbourne office has grown to be an essential component of our international network, carrying on the high standards set by our Colombo branch.
